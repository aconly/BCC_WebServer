What will you do?

By following each step, you will be able to upload a "hash" of a document into the Blockchain. By this you can always prove that:

  1. Your are the owner of this document
  2. When you uploaded this document

How will you do it?

  1. You will create your "Public Address on the Blockchain". You will have a "Private Key" to prove that this address is yours. You will have a secret phrase to re-build the key should you lose it.
  2. You will create a unique number from your document and upload it to the Blockchain. Any change to the document will result in a different number and the change can be detected
  3. You will run a test, to prove the document is stored on the Blockchain and it is real!
